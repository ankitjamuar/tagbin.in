Hero

Auto Expo 2016

Hero MotoCorp Ltd. (Formerly Hero Honda Motors Ltd.) is the world's largest manufacturer of two - wheelers, based in India. In 2001, the company achieved the coveted position of being the largest two-wheeler manufacturing company in India and also, the 'World No.1' two-wheeler company in terms of unit volume sales in a calendar year. Hero MotoCorp Ltd. continues to maintain this position till date.

  • Client: Hero
  • Venue: Expo Mart, Greater Noida
  • Website: www.heromotocorp.com

Challenge

Hero MotoCorp, the world’s largest two-wheeler manufacturer, had recently introduced its new range of engines that were created using state-of-the-art technology. With a vision to provide visitors an impactful experience of the new technology, along with an aim to stand out from the competition, the two-wheeler leader wanted to project its gigantic image at their pavilion in the Auto Expo’16

Solution

The association with Hero allowed the Team Tagbin to explore its limits. The solution provided was to use the core parts of a motorcycle engine to create a large Main Backdrop wall of the stall with 3584 Karizma motorcycle pistons. Thepneumatically coded to-and-fro movement of the pistons was well choreographed to create visual delights for the audience, making brand logos, different shapes, patterns and messages, and was supplemented by a dance performance in front of this brilliantly crafted wall. While this massive imagery was being planned, it was important to simultaneously build an emotional connect with the audience, for which a social media integrated Digital Heart was installed.

Impact

The pavilion, with its major highlights of the Kinetic wall and the Digital Heart, was a runaway success making Hero the most visited brand at the Auto Expo’16. It helped Hero differentiate itself from its competition, elegantly showcasing theits alignment to futuristic technologies as well as building a bond with the consumer. The success tasted even sweeter when Hero MotoCorp won the best two-wheeler pavilion award at the ongoing Auto Expo.

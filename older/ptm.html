About Project Task management

Projects normally pose a huge challenge of barely meeting deadlines, with scattered information and unclear responsibilities. Cutting through this chaos with endless meetings and micromanagement, can only lead to less time at hand to execute projects flawlessly.

Tagbin’s mission is to help businesses excel by enabling teams to work together effortlessly. We help define clear roles & responsibilities, charting definitive timelines, providing clarity, transparency, and focus amongst team members and allowing them to collaborate with less friction and produce great results. The tasks are defined with dependencies of other teams and members helping them track their work and related project timelines. With tasks, projects, team conversations and dashboards, it allows teams to operate meticulously from start to finish, hit deadlines, run productive meetings, and track everything—whether its tasks or money matters.

Rastey

A cab aggregator which provides premium service to the customers

  • Client: Rastey
  • Website: www.rastey.com

Challenge

To develop the core technology which enabled Rastey to keep track of their business data and also seamlessly manage their decision making about driver assignments, cab records and invoicing. Moreover, they also needed an App that facilitates services to its customers anytime & anywhere.

Solution

We created a holistic ecosystem for Rastey which Provided a platform for

  • Rastey to manage its internal system.
  • Associated corporate clients to manage their employee bookings.
  • Nationwide retail users to book cars anytime , anywhere.
A user can simultaneously manage his corporate & personal account.

Impact

Through this solution, Tagbin was able to touch the core of Rastey’s business and developed a user friendly web platform also resulted in more lead generation for the company.
